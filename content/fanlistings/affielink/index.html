How to use it!

All you need to do is download the file, put it in the main folder of your website, and add your affiliates into it! It looks best if you use 75x50 codes, but it should work, no matter the size!

Paste this wherever you want the AffieLink widget to go, and there you go!

About AffieLink!

AffieLink was HEAVILY inspired by Fans Fans Fans! I liked the idea of rotating banner links, and I've always liked the idea of sharing links to other websites I like, so I combined the two. However, I didn't want this to be something people need to sign up for - instead, I wanted to make it into something that anyone who wants it can use.

This way, you can make your own banner rotation to share and promote your affiliates! Technically speaking, you can put links to anything you want, but I made it with fanlisting collective affiliates in mind :)
