About Me!!

Hello hello!! Call me Bailey or Jay, whichever you'd like! I'm , a songwriter, and the webmaster of this site! I've made MANY sites, but this one's my internet home!

As can be expected from that, my main interests at the moment are music and web dev, but there are lots more things that I'm interested in! I like drawing, video editing, baking, and I also love hanging around in fandom spaces! I'm usually behind on my favorite shows, but I still love them!!

I have lots of things that I love to talk about, but the lovely combination of autism and anxiety makes it hard to do so, but that's yet another reason why I'm making this site!! This site is meant as a space to talk about the things I like, but I hope it's enjoyable for anyone who comes to visit!

Thanks for taking a look at this little about page! To show my appreciation, I now offer you a cookie.
